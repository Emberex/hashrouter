Tabbed Browsing Support

HashRouter links are regular links without the need to catch click events. You can middle-click or however else you would normally open links in a new tab. The current page won't change, and the new tab will have the expected content from the link.

Try it by changing the background color of this circle:

  • red
  • green
  • blue
  • random

Change it to whatever color you want by editing the url.
